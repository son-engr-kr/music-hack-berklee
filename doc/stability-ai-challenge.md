# Stability AI Challenge — Stable Audio 3.0

**Prize:** $500 per participant (up to $2,000) for the winning team

## Overview

Stable Audio 3 is a family of open-weights models that generates full songs, solo instruments, and sound effects, with fast inference on consumer hardware and support for personal customization.

## Challenge

Create a publicly available and accessible tool for music producers using Stable Audio 3, encouraging open development and showing the strengths of local open models.

## What Teams Can Build

- Interactive drum machine / sampler with generated samples
- Integration with existing tools (e.g. Max for Live, VSTs)
- Personal streaming radio station
- Personal LoRA trainer to fit the model to your own sample library
